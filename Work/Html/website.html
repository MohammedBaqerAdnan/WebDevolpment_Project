We Provide More Than One Type Of Questions!

Multiple Choices

Admin can make users take MCQ questions, where answers are presented with a list of options, from which they are required to choose one or more accurate answers.

Yes or No

Easy questions that can be answered with either a "yes" or "no" designed to get a quick response from the user, and it does not need any kind of admin revision for correcting answers.

Likert Scale

A scale for rating used to evaluate users' opinions, and perceptions about a specific topic or statement.

Short Answers

Questions that demand a succinct and precise reply, typically comprising a sentence or a few words, and it is adviced for the admin to review the answers in case of any spelling error or bad input from users.
